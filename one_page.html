A new story

This has nothing to do with Mickey and his broccoli obsession.

An unlikely friend

Mickey met Goofy in a dumpster. He wrote a poem:

Oh Goofy
Why are you eating garbage
again?

Well, the point was, Goofy was not ashamed. He had once said: Garbage is beautiful.

Goofy was, in fact, a proud member of GEA. He had even written a book about it: Why We Should All Eat Garbage. In some circles, he was known as a garbagist, a fine connoisseur of inedible the most delicious trash.

Mickey was disappointed because:

  1. Goofy stank;
  2. he was hoping to find some broccolis in the dumpster.

Some words of knowledge

According to Goofy, there were three kinds of garbage:

Tasty
The trash that you want to eat.
Nutritious
The trash that you need to eat.
Taken
No longer available
The trash that you have already eaten.

"You should really check out this website," said Goofy. "It's pretty dope. Also, feel free to keep in touch by email." He then left, headed towards a mysterious destination.
